# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Bubbles is for one person capturing thoughts throughout the day: fragments, advice, reminders, observations, and decisions that would otherwise be sent to themselves in WhatsApp. They need fast capture first, then a way to recover what they saved without scrolling through an endless thread.

## Product Purpose

Bubbles replaces the self-chat habit with a private, local-first memory surface. Success means opening the app, saving a thought in seconds, and later asking a question that returns useful answers grounded in the saved bubbles.

## Brand Personality

Calm, lucid, intimate. The interface should feel like a quiet companion for thinking, with intelligence that stays grounded and out of the way until asked.

## Anti-references

Avoid corporate AI dashboards, generic SaaS panels, fake metrics, over-explaining, productivity theater, and a literal WhatsApp clone. Borrow the speed of chat, not the visual baggage of messaging apps.

## Design Principles

- Capture should always be the shortest path.
- Memory should feel trustworthy because it shows what it used.
- Intelligence should be grounded in saved thoughts, not theatrical.
- Privacy should be clear without turning the app into a warning label.
- The UI should stay quiet enough for personal material.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, semantic form labels, and reduced-motion support. The app should remain usable on small screens without overlapping text or hidden controls.
